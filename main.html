About

I am a passionate trailblazer at the crossroads of machine learning, data science, and communication studies, armed with formidable expertise in advanced analytical methodologies, statistical modeling, and cutting-edge computational frameworks. My mission is to harness the power of natural language processing (NLP) to decode vast troves of textual data from both legacy and emerging media, while diving deep into social media engagement metrics to unlock transformative insights. My research ignites at the frontier of evolving language and framing strategies, driving a relentless pursuit to unravel how these forces shape public opinion and reshape information dynamics across diverse digital landscapes. I am committed to exposing thematic shifts, sentiment patterns, and narrative architectures within media content, while illuminating the pivotal role of media framing in molding audience perceptions and revolutionizing communication ecosystems. My academic goal is to become a professional communication data scientist, leveraging my skills to drive impactful innovation at the intersection of data and communication.
